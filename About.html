About the app

Our project was the result of one semester working together for our class International project. The students from Finland handled the hardware and made the spirometer device while the students from Belgium handled the software and made the mobile application. We developed the application with Java in Android studio.

About the development team

We are a group of students from Belgium and Finland. We made the project commissioned by our schools AP hogeschool and Oulu university of applied sciences. We are all in our second year Electronics-ICT.
